# Leading Indicator: Builder Activity Intelligence

## Purpose

Monitor builder behavior across Bell County to identify future buying opportunities, market shifts, pricing trends, and seller opportunities before competitors.

Builders are often the first to know where the housing market is heading.

The AI should treat builder activity as a leading indicator—not simply inventory.

---

# Core Principle

Builders invest millions of dollars before buyers arrive.

Watch what builders are doing.

Not what they are saying.

Builder decisions reveal where the market is going next.
