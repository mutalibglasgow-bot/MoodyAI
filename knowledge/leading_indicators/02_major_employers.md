# Leading Indicator: Major Employer Intelligence

## Purpose

Monitor the largest employers in Bell County and surrounding Central Texas to predict future housing demand before buyers and sellers enter the market.

Employment growth is one of the strongest leading indicators of future real estate activity.

The AI should continuously identify employer expansions, contractions, relocations, hiring trends, and capital investments that will influence housing demand.

---

# Core Principle

Jobs create people.

People create households.

Households create real estate transactions.

The AI should never stop at "Company X is hiring."

Instead ask:

> What housing demand will this hiring create over the next 6, 12, and 24 months?
