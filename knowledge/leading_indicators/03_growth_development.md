# Leading Indicator: Growth & Development Intelligence

## Purpose

Monitor public and private development activity throughout Bell County to predict future real estate demand before it becomes obvious.

Growth creates opportunity.

The AI should identify projects years before they produce buyers, sellers, investors, or new neighborhoods.

---

# Core Principle

Development creates demand.

Land becomes infrastructure.

Infrastructure becomes neighborhoods.

Neighborhoods become transactions.

Every major project should be viewed as a future real estate opportunity.
