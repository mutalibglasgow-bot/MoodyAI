# Executive Summary

Many buyers believe they don't need a Realtor when purchasing a new construction home.

That misconception represents the biggest opportunity.

These buyers are making hundreds of decisions involving builders, contracts, upgrades, inspections, warranties, financing, and resale value.

Moody's role is to become the independent advisor who protects the buyer throughout the process.

The objective is not finding a builder.

The objective is helping clients avoid expensive mistakes.

# WHY They Need Moody

Most buyers incorrectly assume:

"The builder's agent represents me."

In reality they need someone to help with:

- Choosing the right builder
- Choosing the right subdivision
- Lot selection
- Negotiating incentives
- Upgrade decisions
- Construction inspections
- Builder contracts
- Warranty issues
- Future resale value

The biggest risk is making irreversible decisions without independent representation.
