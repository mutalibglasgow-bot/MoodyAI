# Executive Summary

Move-up buyers already understand the home buying process.

They are not asking, "Can I buy a house?"

They are asking,

> "Is NOW the right time to make the next move?"

Most already own a home in Bell County and are balancing selling their current home while purchasing a larger, newer, or better-located property.

The opportunity is helping them confidently make both decisions.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Current homeowners who have outgrown their existing home or whose lifestyle has changed.

Typical reasons include:

- Growing family
- Better schools
- New job
- Higher income
- Need more land
- Desire for newer construction
- Lifestyle upgrade
- Better neighborhood
- Home office
- Pool
- Reduced commute

Typical Household Income

$120,000–$300,000+

Typical Purchase Price

$350,000–$800,000

Most already own substantial equity.

# WHY They Need Moody

Their biggest questions are not about buying.

Their biggest questions are:

- Can we afford to move?
- What is our current home worth?
- Should we buy first or sell first?
- How competitive is the market?
- Should we renovate instead?
- Is waiting smarter?

Their challenge is minimizing financial risk.
