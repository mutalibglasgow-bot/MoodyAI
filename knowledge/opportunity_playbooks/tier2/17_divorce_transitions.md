# Executive Summary

Divorce is one of the most emotionally difficult events a person experiences.

Buying or selling a home during divorce is rarely about maximizing profit.

It is about creating stability, protecting financial interests, and helping both parties move forward.

Moody's role is not to become part of the conflict.

His role is to become the calm, objective professional everyone trusts.
