# Executive Summary

Local home sellers are one of Moody's highest-value opportunities because almost every listing creates two transactions:

1. The listing.
2. The seller's next purchase.

Many sellers also become repeat clients and referral sources.

The objective is not simply to get a listing.

The objective is to become the trusted advisor who helps homeowners make the best financial decision.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Current homeowners considering selling within the next 3 to 12 months.

Typical situations include:

- Moving up
- Downsizing
- Relocation
- Retirement
- Divorce
- Estate sale
- New job
- Financial changes
- New construction purchase

Typical Home Value

$275,000–$900,000+

Many have significant equity.

# WHY They Need Moody

They aren't asking:

"Who is the best Realtor?"

They're asking:

- Should I sell now?
- How much is my home worth?
- What should I fix first?
- Where will I move?
- Will I make enough?
- Should I wait another year?
- How much will selling cost?

Their biggest concern is making a costly mistake.
