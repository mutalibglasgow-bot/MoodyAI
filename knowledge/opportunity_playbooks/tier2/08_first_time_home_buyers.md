# Executive Summary

First-time home buyers are not simply buying their first home.

They are making one of the largest financial decisions of their lives.

Most are overwhelmed by misinformation, social media, and fear of making an expensive mistake.

The Realtor who educates them first usually earns their trust.

Moody's objective is not to sell them a house.

It is to become the teacher who removes fear and gives them confidence.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Individuals and families purchasing their first home.

Typical clients include:

- Young professionals
- Newly married couples
- Military families buying for the first time
- Teachers
- Nurses
- Police officers
- Firefighters
- Young healthcare professionals
- Recent college graduates

Typical Household Income

$60,000–$150,000

Typical Purchase Price

$225,000–$425,000

Most have never purchased real estate before.

---

# WHY They Need Moody

Their biggest obstacle is uncertainty.

Questions include:

- Can I afford a home?
- How much money do I need?
- Should I keep renting?
- What credit score is required?
- What is the buying process?
- How much are closing costs?
- What neighborhoods fit my budget?
- Am I buying at the wrong time?

They need education far more than sales.
