# Executive Summary

Retirees relocating to Central Texas are not simply buying another house.

They are deciding where they will spend the next twenty to thirty years of their lives.

This decision affects healthcare, taxes, family relationships, recreation, finances, and overall quality of life.

The opportunity is not selling them a home.

The opportunity is helping them confidently choose Bell County as the place they want to call home.

Individuals and couples relocating to Central Texas after retirement.

Typical clients include:

- Military retirees

- Physicians retiring from practice
- Business owners
- Government employees
- Empty nesters
- Couples relocating closer to family

$90,000–$350,000+
