# Executive Summary

Empty nesters are entering one of the biggest transitions of their adult lives.

For the first time in decades, they are making housing decisions based on what *they* want instead of what their children need.

Many are financially secure, emotionally attached to their current home, and looking for a lifestyle upgrade—not necessarily a smaller home.

The opportunity is helping them design the next chapter of life.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Homeowners whose children have recently moved out or are preparing to leave home.

Typical situations include:

- Last child leaving for college
- Children moving away
- Retirement approaching
- Desire to travel
- Looking for less maintenance
- Desire to be closer to grandchildren
- Lifestyle upgrade
- Looking for their "forever home"

Typical Age

50–70

Typical Household Income

$100,000–$300,000+

Typical Home Value

$350,000–$1,000,000+

Many have substantial equity.

Many have lived in the same home for decades.
