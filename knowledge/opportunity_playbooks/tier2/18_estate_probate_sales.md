# Executive Summary

Estate and probate sales are unlike traditional real estate transactions.

Clients are often dealing with grief, legal responsibilities, multiple heirs, financial uncertainty, and homes that may not have been updated in decades.

The real estate transaction is only one piece of a much larger process.

Moody's role is to simplify the entire transition while becoming the trusted advisor for the family and their professional team.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Individuals responsible for handling the sale of property after the death of a homeowner.

Typical clients include:

- Executors
- Estate Administrators
- Surviving spouses
- Adult children
- Trustees
- Attorneys representing estates
- Families managing inherited property

Typical Home Value

$250,000–$1,200,000+

Property Condition

Varies from move-in ready to complete renovation.

# WHY They Need Moody

Most have never handled an estate sale before.

Questions include:

- Do we have to go through probate?
- Can we sell before probate is complete?
- What repairs should we make?
- Should we clean out the home first?
- What is the house worth?
- Should we renovate?
- How do we divide proceeds?
- How long will this take?

Their biggest need is guidance.
