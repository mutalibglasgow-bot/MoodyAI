# Executive Summary

Downsizers are not looking for less house.

They are looking for **more freedom**.

Many have lived in their current home for 15–40 years and are overwhelmed by the thought of selling, sorting possessions, and deciding where to live next.

The opportunity is to become their trusted transition advisor—not just their Realtor.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Homeowners who no longer need or want the size, maintenance, or expense of their current home.

Typical situations include:

- Children have moved out
- Retirement approaching
- Home maintenance becoming difficult
- Desire to simplify life
- Lower monthly expenses
- Move closer to grandchildren
- Health concerns
- Lock-and-leave lifestyle

Typical Age

55–75

Typical Household Income

$90,000–$250,000

Typical Home Value

$350,000–$900,000+

Many own their homes outright or have substantial equity.

---

# WHY They Need Moody

Their challenge isn't selling.

Their challenge is changing a lifestyle they've built over decades.

Questions include:

- Is it really time to downsize?
- Where should we move?
- Should we build?
- How much equity will we have?
- What do we do with everything we own?
- Can we stay close to family?
- Will we regret moving?

This is an emotional transition first.

A real estate transaction second.
