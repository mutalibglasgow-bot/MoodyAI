# Executive Summary

Growing families are one of Bell County's strongest long-term client opportunities.

Unlike first-time buyers, these families already understand homeownership. Their motivation is not buying another house—it is creating a better life for their children.

Their decision is driven by space, safety, schools, and quality of life.

The Realtor who helps them make the best family decision earns trust that often lasts for decades.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Families whose current home no longer fits their lifestyle.

Typical situations include:

- First child
- Second or third child
- Teenagers needing more space
- Working from home
- Homeschooling
- Need for a larger yard
- Better schools
- Safer neighborhood
- Multi-generational living

Typical Household Income

$100,000–$250,000

Typical Purchase Price

$325,000–$700,000

Most already own a home.

Many have significant equity.

# WHY They Need Moody

Their biggest concern isn't square footage.

Questions include:
