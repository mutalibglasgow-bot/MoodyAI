# Executive Summary

Not every high-income healthcare professional relocating to Bell County works for Baylor Scott & White.

Dentists, veterinarians, specialists, CRNAs, pharmacists, physical therapists, administrators, and private practice physicians relocate to Central Texas every year.

These clients often have strong incomes, long careers ahead of them, and become exceptional long-term referral sources.

The opportunity is to become Bell County's relocation expert for healthcare professionals—not just BSW physicians.
