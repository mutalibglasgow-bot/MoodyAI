# Executive Summary

Enlisted military families relocating to Fort Cavazos represent one of the largest and most consistent sources of residential real estate transactions in Bell County. These families often relocate on compressed timelines, have young children, operate within defined budgets, and rely heavily on trusted recommendations.

The opportunity is not simply helping them buy a house.

The opportunity is becoming the trusted guide that makes one of the most stressful moves in their military career easier.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Active-duty enlisted military personnel and their families relocating to Fort Cavazos under Permanent Change of Station (PCS) orders.

Typical ranks include:

- E4 Specialists / Corporals
- E5 Sergeants
- E6 Staff Sergeants
- E7 Sergeants First Class
- E8 Master Sergeants
- E9 Sergeants Major

Many are:

- Married
- First or second home purchase
- VA Loan eligible
- Parents of young children
- Moving from another state or overseas

Typical Household Income

$65,000–$140,000+

Typical Purchase Price

$225,000–$450,000
