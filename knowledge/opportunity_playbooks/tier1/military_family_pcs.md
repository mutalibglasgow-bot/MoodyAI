# Executive Summary

This client has received Permanent Change of Station (PCS) orders to Fort Cavazos.

Their move is mandatory.

Unlike a typical buyer, they cannot simply wait for a better market. They have a reporting date, a family to relocate, and dozens of important decisions to make in a very short period of time.

Many are moving from another state and know very little about Bell County.

The AI's job is to eliminate uncertainty and become the family's trusted relocation guide before they arrive in Texas.
