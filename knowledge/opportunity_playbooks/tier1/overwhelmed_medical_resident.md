# Executive Summary

This client has just matched into a residency program at Baylor Scott & White Temple.

Their move is exciting—but also stressful.

They are balancing licensing, onboarding, moving logistics, finances, and the start of one of the most demanding periods of their career.

Real estate is only one of many major decisions they must make.

The AI's job is to reduce stress by providing clarity, confidence, and trusted local guidance.
