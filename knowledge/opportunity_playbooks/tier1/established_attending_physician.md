# Executive Summary

This client has accepted an attending physician position in Central Texas.

Unlike a medical resident, this client is making a long-term lifestyle decision rather than simply finding a place to live.

Their priorities extend well beyond commute.

They are evaluating schools, neighborhoods, financial security, lifestyle, long-term appreciation, and the quality of life their family will experience.

The AI should treat this client as someone making one of the largest financial and lifestyle decisions of their career.

The objective is to become their trusted relocation advisor before they begin interviewing Realtors.
