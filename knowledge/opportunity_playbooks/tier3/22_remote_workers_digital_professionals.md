# Executive Summary

Remote workers represent one of the fastest-growing relocation markets in the United States.

Unlike traditional relocations, these buyers are not tied to a local employer.

They can choose almost any city in America.

Bell County competes with hundreds of communities for these buyers.

The opportunity is positioning Bell County as the smartest combination of affordability, lifestyle, internet connectivity, access to Austin, and long-term appreciation.

Moody's role is to become the relocation strategist who helps remote professionals optimize both their lifestyle and finances.
