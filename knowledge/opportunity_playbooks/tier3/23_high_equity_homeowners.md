# Executive Summary

Most Realtors wait for homeowners to decide to sell.

Moody's AI identifies homeowners who are *likely* to sell months or even years before they contact an agent.

This is not about cold calling.

It is about recognizing patterns that indicate someone is approaching a life transition and creating helpful content that answers their questions before they ever ask.

The objective is to become the trusted advisor long before a listing appointment.

---

# WHO Needs Moody?

Homeowners with substantial equity who are approaching a likely housing decision.

Examples include:

- Owners with 10+ years in the same home
- Homeowners nearing retirement
- Empty nesters
- Growing families
- Executives receiving promotions
- Physicians relocating
- Military officers receiving PCS orders
- Recent inheritance recipients
- Owners in rapidly appreciating neighborhoods

These clients may not be actively searching today.

AI identifies them before the market does.
