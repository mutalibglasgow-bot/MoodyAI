# Executive Summary

1031 Exchange clients are among the highest-value clients a Realtor can acquire.

Unlike traditional buyers, these clients are operating under strict IRS deadlines with significant tax implications. A failed exchange can cost tens or hundreds of thousands of dollars in taxes.

The opportunity is not finding replacement property.

The opportunity is becoming the trusted advisor who successfully guides one of the most financially important transactions of their lives.
