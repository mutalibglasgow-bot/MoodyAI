# Executive Summary

New attending physicians represent one of the highest-value opportunities in Bell County.

Unlike residents, attending physicians are making a long-term career decision. They are often purchasing their "forever" home, upgrading significantly in price point, and making decisions that affect their spouse, children, and long-term financial future.

These clients are not simply buying a house.

They are choosing a community.

The goal is to become their trusted relocation advisor before they begin interviewing Realtors.
