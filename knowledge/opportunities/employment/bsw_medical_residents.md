# Executive Summary

Incoming Baylor Scott & White Medical Center residents represent one of the highest-value and most predictable relocation opportunities in Central Texas.

Every year, a new class of physicians arrives in Temple. Nearly all of them must find housing within a short period of time while learning a new city, a demanding job, and often moving with a spouse, partner, pets, or young children.

Most have never lived in Temple before.

Most know very little about Bell County.

The opportunity is not to sell them a house.

The opportunity is to become their trusted relocation advisor.

If Moody becomes the first trusted source they encounter, there is an excellent chance they become lifelong clients and referral sources.
