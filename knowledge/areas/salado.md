# Executive Summary

Salado is Bell County's premier luxury small-town community.

Unlike Temple, which is driven by employment, and Belton, which emphasizes family living, Salado attracts buyers seeking lifestyle, privacy, prestige, and long-term quality of life.

People rarely "accidentally" buy in Salado.

They choose Salado because they want the lifestyle that comes with it.

The AI should position Salado as a premium destination for executives, physicians, retirees, entrepreneurs, and luxury buyers.
